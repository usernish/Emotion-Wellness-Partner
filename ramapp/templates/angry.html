Task 1: Engage in relaxation techniques

Physical exercise has been shown to improve mood and reduce feelings of rage. Try the following activities:

  • Pause for a moment and take a few slow, deep breaths to relax and center yourself
  • Hydrate yourself by drinking a refreshing glass of water.
  • Do a quick stretching routine to release tension and improve flexibility.

Task 2: Practice Mindfulness Techniques

Mindfulness Techniques can help reduce stress and promote a sense of calmness. Consider the following practices:

  • Spend a few minutes decluttering and organizing your workspace for better focus and productivity
  • Jot down three achievable goals you'd like to accomplish today.
  • Take a short walk around your neighborhood to get some fresh air and clear your mind.

Task 3:Improve Communication and Conflict Resolution

Social support and connection with loved ones can provide comfort and uplift your mood. Take the following steps:

  • Make a quick phone call to connect with a family member or friend you haven't spoken to in a while.
  • Share a friendly smile with someone you pass by or encounter during your day.
  • Practice saying no to additional commitments or tasks that may lead to stress.
